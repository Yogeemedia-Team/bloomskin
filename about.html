About Us

Cosmetic & Beauty Store

We Make Beauty

Bloom Skin Clinic is a Specialist Medical Center located in Madiwela, Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte (Sri Lanka).

Primarily focusing on Dermatology it serves as a general medical treatment centre as well. The center operates under the leadership of Dr. (Mrs.) Saroja De Silva, a medical doctor working in the field of dermatology for over 10 years. We treat patients of all ages and from all communities on skin issues ranging from teenage acne, infections, rashes, fungal infections, psoriasis, and pigmentation, to adult hair loss.

We provide tailor-made treatment plans based on your symptoms and condition. Bloom Skin Clinic: We look forward to helping you get healthy!
